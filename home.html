Our Mission

There are over 1.5 million non-profits in the United States. As an average person, it can often be difficult deciding which ones are worthwile contributing to. Which non-profits are spending their money wisely? Which ones do the most good for society?

We want to provide people with the information they need to make informed choices about non-profits around the United States. Everybody needs to know whether or not their time or money will be wasted if they choose to make a contribution.
